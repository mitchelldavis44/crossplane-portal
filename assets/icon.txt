Icon Design Concept:

A circular icon with:
- A blue gradient background (#1E88E5 to #0D47A1)
- A white portal/gateway symbol in the center
- Three interconnected cloud shapes around the portal
- A subtle grid pattern in the background

The icon should be:
- Clean and modern
- Professional looking
- Easily recognizable at small sizes
- Reflective of both portal/management and cloud-native concepts

Color Scheme:
- Primary Blue: #1E88E5
- Dark Blue: #0D47A1
- White: #FFFFFF
- Light Blue Accent: #64B5F6

This design represents:
- The portal/gateway symbol for the portal aspect
- Cloud shapes for the cloud-native nature of Crossplane
- Interconnected elements for resource management
- Blue color scheme for trust and technology
